Innovate. Create. Connect.

ACM VVIT Nambur is the student chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery at Vasireddy Venkatadri Institute of Technology, Guntur. We bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world applications while fostering a community of tech enthusiasts and innovators.

Explore Join Us

What We Do

ACM VVIT Nambur bridges the gap between academic learning and real-world applications through a variety of technical activities and community initiatives.

Technical Workshops

Hands-on workshops on cutting-edge technologies, helping bridge the gap between academic learning and industry applications.

SPARDHA Tech Fest

Our flagship tech fest featuring Algo Arena, Treasure Troopers, Game Of Codes, and Riddle Rampage competitions.

ACM-W Initiatives

Special programs focused on empowering and supporting women in computing and technology fields.

Blind 'C' Challenge

An innovative coding competition that tests specific programming skills and problem-solving abilities.

Professional Development

Opportunities to enhance technical skills, network with professionals, and stay updated on the latest industry trends.

Community Building

A collaborative environment where students share knowledge, exchange ideas, and work together on innovative projects.

Our Journey

Explore the evolution of ACM VVIT Nambur and our significant milestones throughout the years.

2012

Chapter Foundation

ACM VVIT Student Chapter was established with a vision to promote computing as a science and profession.

2016

First SPARDHA Tech Fest

Successfully organized our first major tech festival that has now become our flagship annual event.

2018

ACM-W Chapter Launched

Established our Women in Computing chapter to focus on promoting gender diversity in technology.

2022

Expanded College Participation

Extended our reach to 36 engineering colleges across Andhra Pradesh for our events and programs.

2024

Hour of Code Initiative

Introduced coding to schools across the region as part of ACM's global Hour of Code movement.
